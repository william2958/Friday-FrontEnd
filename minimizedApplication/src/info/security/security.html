Your PIN
PIN:

Your 6-digit alpha-numeric pin is the key used to encrypt and decrypt all of the information that is stored in our database.

Your pin is never kept in any database, in fact, it never leaves the browser you're on right now. This ensures that the only person that can read your data, is you.

Why can't I reset my PIN?

This is why we cannot change or recover your pin, there is simply nothing to change. Since we don't store your pin anywhere, if you forget your pin, we would be unable to recover any of your data.

This is why it is very important to choose a pin you're going to remember! Having a pin that only you keep track of is how we keep your information safe and secure.

Security
Encryption

Keeping your data safe and secure is one of the primary concerns for F.R.I.D.A.Y.

We use state of the art encryption methods to help protect your data. All of your confidential information is rigorously encrypted with your personal pin before it even leaves your browser.

AES-256 Encryption is used to encrypt all your passwords with your personal key. AES-256 is a secure encryption method that is used with most secure datasets. It turns your legible passwords into indecyphrable numbers. This process is irreversible without the key.

"password" -> "[235,430,50,12,403,23]"

With our best in class security, you can rest assured that you are the only person who can see your information.

Fun Facts:

- Breaking the AES-256 encryption using the world's fastest computers would take over a billion, billion years.

- Since there are 62 possible values for each digit of your pin, there are more than 56 billion possible pin combinations.
